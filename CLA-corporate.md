## 1. Definitions

**"You" (or "Your")** means the corporation or other legal entity on behalf of which this Agreement is executed.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted by you or your Designated Employees to the Project for inclusion in the Project. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Source code
- Documentation
- Configuration files
- Bug reports containing code samples or patches
- Feature requests containing code samples
- Any other material submitted via pull request, issue, email, or other communication channels

**"Submit"** means any form of electronic, verbal, or written communication sent to the Project, including but not limited to communication on electronic mailing lists, source code control systems, and issue tracking systems.

**"Designated Employee"** means an employee, contractor, or agent of your organization who is authorized by you to make Contributions on your behalf. Designated Employees are identified in Schedule A or through a written amendment to this Agreement.

**"Project"** means FrogDB and related software maintained at the Project's official repositories.
